An Opportunity for Aspiring Grades 6-11 Scholars
The SPH Breakthrough Scholarship offers a transformative educational opportunity for talented Indonesian students in Grades 6-11, providing access to SPH's outstanding learning environment regardless of their financial background.
If your child demonstrates exceptional academic achievement and is entering Grades 6 through 11* in the 2027/2028 academic year, we warmly invite you to register your interest. This prestigious scholarship program covers school fees from the student's entry grade through to graduation in Grade 12.
Our Breakthrough Scholarship program is designed for high-achieving students who are driven to make a significant impact. Express your interest today and embark on your journey to becoming an #sphscholar.
For detailed information regarding the specific types of scholarships offered and the grade levels available at each campus for the upcoming academic year, please refer to our FAQ .

Student Dormitory Scholarship At SPH Sentul City
The SPH Breakthrough Scholarship extends an exceptional opportunity for talented students across the archipelago to access a premier education at SPH.
In addition to financial education support, our scholarship program also includes partial funding (10%) for student accommodation within the Pelita Harapan House (PHH) at our SPH Sentul City campus in the Bogor region. Here, students are invited to immerse themselves in a unique learning and living environment where verdant outdoor spaces seamlessly complement structured, supervised study sessions.

More than just a residence, our dormitory serves as a nurturing second home where students cultivate academic excellence and gain invaluable life skills, preparing them thoroughly for the demands of university and their future endeavors.

Talent Scholarship Pathway at SPH Kemang Village
This year, SPH Kemang Village opens Talent Scholarship, a non-academic scholarship pathway designed for students who demonstrate outstanding abilities and achievements in areas beyond the academics.
This pathway is intended to support students who consistently pursue excellence and positively contribute to their community. Students with talents in sports, music, arts and design, performing arts, debate & public speaking, technology and innovation, and other recognized talents aligned with the SPH student profile, are encouraged to apply.

SCHOLARSHIP REQUIREMENTS & APPLICATION PROCESS
Find out the required documents for your scholarship application here
Learn more
Required documents for your scholarship application:
  • Report card from the following academic years:
    • 2024-2025
    • 2025-2026
    • 2026-2027
  • A scholarship application letter signed by parents, which includes:
    • Reasons for applying to the SPH scholarship program.
    • Reasons your child should be awarded a scholarship at SPH.
    • The expected amount of scholarship received based on your affordability.
  • Student Essay, addressing the writing prompt,
    • Academic Scholarship: “If you could change one thing in the world today, what would it be? How would you do it and why?”
    • Non-Academic Scholarship:: “What do you think is your talent and how have you been using it and hope to continue use it to impact others around you?”
  • List of academic and non-academic achievements.
    We appreciate scholars who performed excellently in both academic and non-academic areas. If your child actively participates in any activities inside or outside the school, please list and submit any documentation related to awards or certificates they have ever received. For students applying for non-academic pathway at SPH Kemang Village, a minimum of a national-level achievement or documentation must be submitted.
Learn about the 7 simple steps you will need to take to be an SPH Breakthrough Scholar
Learn more
To apply for our scholarship program, please complete the following steps:
  • Submit The Application Form
    Complete and submit your scholarship application form through our online application portal.
  • Indicate Scholarship Interest
    Ensure you select ’YES’ when prompted with the question regarding scholarship interest within the application form.
  • Settle The Registration Fee
    Pay the adjusted registration fee with a 50% reduction during the application period.
  • Upload Required Documents
    Upload all the required documents before the application deadline listed on the timeline section of this page.
  • Await Test Details
    Once all documents are verified, our Admissions team will contact you to schedule your scholarship test. The test will cover the following:
    • Bahasa Indonesia (at several campuses)
    • English
    • Mathematics
    • Applicant’s interview
  • Complete Scholarship Interview
    After or during the scholarship test day, applicants will need to do an interview with the scholarship committee. However, its schedule might differ at each campus.
